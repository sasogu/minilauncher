Minilauncher is a minimalist Android launcher designed to reduce friction, distractions, and impulsive phone use.

The interface focuses on showing only what is necessary:

- clock and date on the main screen
- visual battery ring
- fixed shortcuts for phone and camera
- persistent favorites on the home screen
- separate screen for the full app list

It includes a brief mindful pause before opening apps, where you can indicate how long you plan to use each one. If you choose a duration, the app schedules a local reminder to help you stay aware of your screen time.

It includes no trackers, no ads, and no Google Play Services dependencies.
